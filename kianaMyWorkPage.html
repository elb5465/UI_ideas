MyRecipes

An attempt to push forward the digitization of recipe cards and books.

A two month long research study focused on digitizing conventional recipe books and recipe cards.

MyRecipes allows individuals to upload, store, and access recipes all from one place. Recipes can be uploaded from scratch, from a website, and from a photo.

02.

Friend

for Life

Designing a search experience to
find you the perfect rescue pet.

A two month long research study that focused on helping prospective pet owners search for the perfect rescue animal.

Friend for Life, an animal shelter based in the United States, features multiple device compatibility, pet filters, meet and greet scheduling with email confirmation, and donation and volunteer based support.

Friend for Life allows prospective pet owners to search for their ideal rescue pet using various filters. For those who are not looking to adopt but are looking to support the animal shelter, opportunities to donate and volunteer were also made available.
